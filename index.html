Benfica FC

Welcome to Benfica FC, the premier football club in Kannangatt, Ramanthali, Kerala, India. Founded in 2012, we have quickly become a beloved and respected club in our local community. With over 150 members, we are proud to offer a supportive and inclusive environment for players of all skill levels. Whether you're an experienced player or just starting out, we have a place for you on our team. At Benfica FC, we are dedicated to excellence both on and off the field. We have won numerous domestic trophies and are always striving to improve our game. But our commitment to our community is just as important as our love of football.
